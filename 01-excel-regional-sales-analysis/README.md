# Regional Sales Analysis (Excel)

## Business Question
How is U.S. sales revenue distributed across regions, and which regions contribute most to total revenue?

## Dataset
Superstore Sales Dataset (2015â€“2019)

## Tools
Excel (PivotTable, % of total, conditional formatting, chart)

## Key Insight
West and East regions together generate ~61% of total U.S. sales, indicating strong revenue concentration in these markets. The South contributes the smallest share at ~17%.

## Recommendation
Prioritize investment and marketing efforts in the West and East regions while investigating drivers of underperformance in the South.
